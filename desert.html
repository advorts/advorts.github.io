Camel Safari

Camel Safari mainly takes place in Rajasthan, which is a state located in the northwest region of India. The state of Rajasthan provides local attractions for tourists and travellers in Aravalis, in the vast Thar Desert, which is known as one of the oldest mountain ranges in the world.

Other locations for enjoying Camel Safari are the forts, temples and palaces established by Rajput kings such as Bappa Rawal, Rana Kumbha, Rana Sanga and Rana Pratap.

Desert Jeep safari

The fastest and most comfortable way to travel and explore the beauty of the desert is through Jeep Safari. Travelling by a Jeep through a desert is considered a smoother and comfortable ride according to a lot of locals and avid travellers of the region.

One of the popular places for Jeep Safari is Jaisalmer, Rajasthan. Jeep Safari is one of the many reasons why tourists love to go in Thar Desert Jaisalmer. The various attractions and the beautiful surroundings can be better enjoyed through a Jeep.

Sandboarding

Sandboarding is very much similar to snowboarding. The only difference is that it takes place on sand dunes rather than snow-covered mountains. This sport can only be performed in desert areas or coastal areas. It requires little bit of learning and practice to master this sport.

Rajasthan in India provides best deserts for Sandboarding.
